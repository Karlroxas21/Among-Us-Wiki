Accounts


Accounts are a feature in Among Us. All players must follow the Innersloth Code of Conduct which ensures a safe, enjoyable, and encouraging space for players to play Innersloth games.

A message shown prompting the user to sign in.

Features

  • Players are able to report if someone in the game is acting inappropriately, including: inappropriate names, inappropriate chat, cheating/hacking, and harassment/misconduct. Consequences can range from temporary to permanent bans (including alternate accounts).
  • Accessing Free Chat requires an account (without it the only chat option is Quick Chat).
  • Accounts are required to change the player's display name. If the player does not create an account, there is a button that can be used to randomize the player's display name.
    • Usernames are not exclusive. Multiple people can have the same username.
    • If multiple players with the same username join the same lobby, each player with a matching username after the first to join is numbered next to their name until they leave the game.
  • If the player is under the minimum age of digital consent in their country (for most countries 13 is the minimum age), they will need a parent/guardian's permission to create an account.
  • Without an account the player can still play via Guest Mode which has social features (only Quick Chat and randomized names).
  • In version 2022.3.29, the accounts system was updated to integrate a Friends List.
  • In version 2021.4.12, the accounts system was updated to add an option to go offline, which makes the Online button inaccessible. Additionally, the accounts tab has a colored outline that changes based on the player's status:
    • Green means that the player is logged into their account.
    • Yellow means that the player is not logged into their account and is playing as a guest.
    • Gray means that the player is playing offline.
    • Blue means that the player is pending confirmation from their parent or guardian to have access to a full account.
  • In version 2021.11.9, the accounts system was updated again to allow one account to be shared across multiple devices. This allows cosmetics and EXP obtained on one device to carry over to another and back again. Accounts may be linked through Innersloth's website.
    • The process of linking a main account to another account overwrites any and all progress from the other account to make way for the main one.
    • Account linking is unavailable on the Nintendo Switch edition and the PlayStation edition.
    • In version 2022.3.29, account linking was temporarily disabled to make sure Friends List work properly.

History

Accounts were confirmed on September 23, 2020, in a devlog by Innersloth as one of the new features that will be implemented into Among Us after the cancellation of the game's sequel, Among Us 2. They stated that a friends system would also be added.

On November 3, 2020, moderation features were announced in the in-game announcements. The release date of this feature was revealed to be sometime during December 2020. However, as of early 2021, the update has still not released.

On November 19, 2020, the Among Us Twitter account made a post stating that moderation features are a top priority. They said, "We want our space to be welcoming & respectful, so this is our first step in sabotaging the toxicity and hacking."

An external development team is working on the accounts system, though it has not been confirmed who the team is.

On March 18, 2021, the first version of accounts was confirmed to be releasing on March 31, 2021, alongside The Airship and released on schedule.


Trivia
