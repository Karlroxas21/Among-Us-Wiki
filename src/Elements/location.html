Locations

Locations in Among Us each have a specific usage. On The Skeld, Polus, and The Airship, entering most locations is only possible when the doors in the location are not Sabotaged. Living Impostors may use vents to quickly travel to many locations, such as Admin.

Overview

Locations are a very important mechanic in Among Us. Locations sometimes make a perfect environment to kill Crewmates, such as Electrical on The Skeld, largely known for the large amount of kills that take place in the latter. A vast majority of locations also contain tasks.



List of Locations:


The Skeld

Admin

On The Skeld, Admin is a small room located under and slightly to the right of Cafeteria with only one exit. There is a security camera outside of it, along with a Fix Wiring panel. A table in the center of the room features both the Admin panel on its left side and the Swipe Card task on its right side.

A vent is situated in the lower-left corner (connected to both Cafeteria and right corridor vents). One of the two O2 keyboards is used to fix the Oxygen Depleted sabotage in the upper right corner. Finally, the upper left corner features the Upload Data task.

Cafeteria

On The Skeld, Cafeteria is the spawn point for players at the start of the game and after an emergency meeting ends. It is the largest location which contains five blue circular tables and has three doors: the left leads to a hallway connecting Upper Engine and MedBay; the right door leads to Weapons; and the bottom door leads to a hallway connecting Storage and Admin. A vent is located near the right door, and the emergency button is located on the middle table. The upper left corner features the Fix Wiring panel, while the upper right features the Empty Garbage and Download Data tasks' panels.

Communications

Communications on The Skeld is a medium-sized room in the lower-right hand corner, located between Storage and Shields. It features two tasks, Download Data and Accept Diverted Power, which are located on the left and right side of the door, respectively, and a radio at the bottom used to fix Comms Sabotaged.

Electrical

On The Skeld, Electrical is a room that has wires running across the floor and walls and other electrical appliances, such as a distributor and a generator. The entrance is located in the middle of the hallway between Storage and Lower Engine. The middle of the room features a panel to fix the Fix Lights Sabotage. At the back, there are panels for all of the tasks located here. There is a vent located under the Download Data panel, which is connected to those of Security and MedBay.

Lower Engine

Lower Engine is a small room on The Skeld, located at the bottom-left corner of the map. There is a vent in the bottom right corner, which leads to Reactor. There is a big engine on the left side, with panels for a couple of the tasks, and Accept Diverted Power is located above, right below the hall. This location is connected to two hallways; one leading to Reactor, Security, and Upper Engine, and one leading to Electrical and Storage.

Medbay

On The Skeld, MedBay is a medium-sized room that contains a vent, a scanner, and four hospital beds. It houses three tasks, and its vent leads to the vents in Security and Electrical.

Navigation

Navigation is a small room located on the far right side of the map, which serves as the ship's control room with various navigation equipment. It contains two vents, with the upper one connecting to Weapons and the lower one connecting to Shields. It has five tasks, four in the main area, and a Fix Wiring panel in the hallway to the left. It is similar to Reactor, with the two locations being at opposites of the map with two vents that lead to the northern and southern rooms.

O2

On The Skeld, O2 is a small room located towards the map's top-right corner, below Weapons. It features three short tasks and one of the number pads to resolve Oxygen Depleted. It appears that a plant is inside the box with the number pad, possibly helping produce oxygen to the ship. To the right of O2 is a camera, which also captures the entrance to Navigation.

Reactor

On The Skeld, Reactor is a room located in the corridor across from Security. It contains two vents, with the upper one connecting to Upper Engine and the lower one connecting to Lower Engine. While the room's Reactor typically glows blue, it will begin to glow red if Reactor Meltdown is happening, with paths to the handprint scanners lighting up. A fake Divert Power panel is shown but cannot be used.

Security

On The Skeld, Security is a small room across from Reactor with a vent in the lower right corner. Above that is a desk and on the north side is a computer where players can access Security. When near Security, ambient sound is heard.

Shields

Shields is a small room located at the far southeastern corner on The Skeld near Navigation and Communications, and contains several lamps with lights at the edges. In addition, a vent is located near the Prime Shields panel, which connects to the southern vent in Navigation. If the lights in the bottom right corner outside the ship are lit, it means at least one Crewmate has completed the Prime Shields visual task.

Storage

On The Skeld, Storage has large containers in the middle of the room, which only allow for movement around the room's walls. On the bottom left of the pile of containers, there is a red gas canister, which is used to refill the fuel can during the Fuel Engines task. It has three doors: up, which leads to Cafeteria, passing Admin; left, which leads to Lower Engine, passing Electrical; and right, which leads to Shields, passing Communications. In the bottom right corner of the room, there is a lever and a hatch where the garbage is flushed away for the second stage of the Empty Garbage task, which is a visual task.

Upper Engine

Upper Engine is a small room on The Skeld that contains three tasks and a single vent. This location is connected to the hallway leading to Reactor and Security, as well as the hallway leading to MedBay and Cafeteria.

Weapons

On The Skeld, Weapons is a medium-sized room located to the right of Cafeteria and above O2. There are two exits: one that leads to Cafeteria and one that leads to a hallway connecting it, O2, Navigation, and Shields. In the center of the room is a chair sitting on a platform with a holographic screen, used for Clear Asteroids. There are also panels for Download Data and Accept Diverted Power, located at the top and to the right, respectively. A vent lies just below the Download Data panel. A turret fires a laser on the outside of the ship every time a player fires a shot in the Clear Asteroids task.

MIRA HQ

Admin

On MIRA HQ, Admin is located under Greenhouse and across from Office. There is a table in the center featuring the Enter Id Code and Prime Shields tasks, as well as the Admin ability. The top wall features two panels for the Accept Diverted Power and Chart Course tasks. A vent is located in the bottom right corner.

Balcony

Balcony is located at the bottom of MIRA HQ, under Cafeteria, with the Clear Asteroids task on the left, the Measure Weather task on the right, and a vent in the middle between the two sliding doors. The sliding doors automatically open for players but do not have collision.

Cafeteria

On MIRA HQ, Cafeteria is the main spawn point for players after an emergency meeting ends. However, it is not where players begin the game. It is located in the right corridor and is smaller than its predecessor. It is also near Storage (located in the right of Cafeteria) and Balcony (under Cafeteria), and the tables are cubic and light orange instead of circular and blue. The emergency button is located on the lower-left table. The upper left wall features a Divert Power panel, while the upper right wall features the Empty Garbage and Buy Beverage tasks. Above Cafeteria, there is an exit leading to Hallway and a vent.

Communications

Communications on MIRA HQ is a medium-sized room at the bottom of the map, above MedBay. Accept Diverted Power, located in the top-left corner, is the only task present. Next to that is a panel used to fix Comms Sabotaged, and besides that, the Doorlog ability, which marks all players that pass through Hallway.

Decontamination

On MIRA HQ, Decontamination is a long hallway that runs from Locker Room to a hallway that separates Reactor and Laboratory. To enter, players must use a button of the doors to open. After some time, the room fills with vapor. When it is clear again, the opposite door opens before closing after a few seconds, and both doors' buttons becoming usable again. There are floor lights of eight segments, which will suggest when the doors will open. First, when the button is pressed, the eight segments' lights will turn off, and the door closes. Then, after the vapor, the eight segments will turn on again, and the next door opens.

Greenhouse

Greenhouse is a tall room with pots full of shrubs, flowers, plants, and a large oxygen filter with a tree inside. The roof contains several dry plants that sway slowly, and a vent is located behind the filter. The floor is entirely made of some transparent material (possibly glass). There are panels on the left and right of Greenhouse for the Accept Diverted Power and the second stage of the Water Plants tasks, respectively. There are also two panels on the oxygen filter used for the Oxygen Depleted Sabotage, as well as the Clean O2 Filter task.

Hallway

Hallway is an upside-down Y-shaped hallway containing Doorlog sensors at each of its exits. The north, bottom-left and bottom-right exits leads to Greenhouse, Locker Room / Communications and Cafeteria respectively. It has one task at its bottom-left, which is Fix Wiring. At its bottom-right near Cafeteria, there is a vent that directly connects to vents in Admin and Balcony. Hallway is one of the busiest locations on MIRA HQ, so it is risky for An Impostor to kill there. However, by using the vent at the bottom-right near Cafeteria correctly, it is possible to escape after a kill.

Laboratory

On MIRA HQ, Laboratory is located across from Reactor, and past Decontamination. It features a one-way window that can only be looked through on the hallway side. Along the top wall are panels for Fix Wiring, Accept Diverted Power, and Assemble Artifact. There is also a vent in the top right corner, directly connected to the vents in Reactor and Decontamination. At the bottom, there is a table used for Sort Samples.

Launchpad

Launchpad is the spawn point for players at the start of a game on MIRA HQ. The outside of Dropship is located here, along with several boxes, a diagnostic computer, another device that serves to fuel the engines, and a panel for Accept Diverted Power in the lower-left corner. In addition, it has a vent in the upper left corner connected to the rest of the vents on the map.

Locker Room

There are two parts to Locker Room:

The hallway side of the room, which contains three sets of four lockers, a bench, and a rug, as well as an additional bench just to the left of Locker Room's entrance from Hallway.

The decontamination side of the room, which contains the entrance to Decontamination and the button to open the door with. A Fix Wiring panel is located to the left of the door into Decontamination.

Medbay

On MIRA HQ, MedBay is a medium-sized room that contains a scanner and seven futuristic hospital beds. It houses three tasks and a vent that directly leads to Locker Room and Balcony.

Office

The Office is a medium-sized room located at the end of the northernmost hallway, across from Admin. It has a vent in the upper left corner. Two tasks are found here. Fix Lights and part of Comms Sabotaged are resolved here.

Reactor

On MIRA HQ, Reactor is connected to Laboratory. It contains a massive reactor with a claw that emits a laser, several computers, and two fingerprint scanners that are used to resolve Reactor Meltdown, similar to The Skeld. The room's Reactor also glows red during Reactor Meltdown. To reach Reactor, Crewmates must first go through Decontamination. A vent is located on the left below, allowing Impostors to enter without going through Decontamination. Several one-way windows can also be seen through from the hallway side of Reactor.

Storage

On this map, Storage is located near Cafeteria. Inside the room is a bookcase which contains old materials, including the watering can, which is used for the Water Plants task. A Fix Wiring panel is located at the exit of the room.

Polus

Admin

On Polus, Admin is located south of Office. Unlike the other two Admin rooms, the Admin room on Polus is much larger and features no tasks. Instead, it features lots of bookshelves and a vent in the bottom-left corner. The right portion of Admin features a table with the Admin ability and a door leading to Decontamination.

Boiler Room

Boiler Room is a small room on Polus. It is located on the bottom left-hand side of the map, south of O2. There are two pipes on the left and right, which are used for two out of three steps of the Open Waterways task, as well as a water jug in the top-left corner for the first stage of Replace Water Jug. In addition, there is a camera outside the south exit of O2 that can be accessed by using Security, which shows the right side of Boiler Room.

Communications

Communications on Polus is a medium-sized room that is not directly connected to any other room but is located above Weapons. At the top-left corner there is a panel for Reboot Wifi, and a computer for Upload Data. To the left of Communications is a panel used to resolve Comms Sabotaged.

Decontamination

On Polus, there are two Decontamination locations, each on either side of two hallways that extend out from Specimen Room, one that goes up to Laboratory, and one that goes left and down to Admin. They are small, square-shaped rooms, and the one accessible from Laboratory has a panel for the Fix Wiring task.

Dropship

On Polus, Dropship acts as the spawn location. It is a dead-end located at the top of the map, slightly left from the middle, and leads directly outside. There is a door in the back with task panels on the left and right, which are used for Chart Course and Insert Keys, respectively.

Electrical

On Polus, Electrical is a room connected to Security and O2, having an exposed area that contains electricity generators and a transparent wire fence. The panel to fix the Fix Lights Sabotage is located in the outside area. There are panels for Download Data and Fix Wiring inside. A vent is located at the end of the corridor, near the Security entrance, and connects to a vent outside Electrical and O2. There is also a security camera outside the right exit of Electrical.

Laboratory

Laboratory on Polus is a large room encompassing several smaller rooms. At the left entrance is a long hallway that connects two sections. The left-most section is an exposed area with a drill used in Repair Drill. The right-most section is the weather station that features a television with the weather forecast on it. In addition, there are two panels along the top wall used for the Record Temperature and the second stage of the Fix Weather Node tasks.

O2

On Polus, O2 consists of three rooms, two medium-sized and one small - and is located above Boiler Room. The top-left room contains the first stage of the Upload Data task, the Monitor Tree task, and a vent. The main room contains the Empty Garbage and Fix Wiring tasks, and the small room contains the Fill Canisters task.

Office

The Office takes up the space of two long rooms: one labeled as "Office", which features the emergency button and the second stage of Replace Water Jug, and another housing Vitals and all the other tasks in Office. During emergency meetings, players spawn in the left part of Office, huddled around a wide, oval-shaped table with the button placed in the middle. The two rooms are connected by a hallway, which also connects to Admin and a door in the bottom left leading Outside.

Outside

Outside is the planetary base's outer area, being located practically from the right exit of the Office, with a lava pit, several pilasters of climatic modules, among other things. A metal plank leads out to the lava used when a player is ejected. However, while playing, players cannot fall into the lava. This area can also be seen through the security cameras.

Security

On Polus, Security is a tiny room linked with Electrical on the upper-left side of the map. It has a computer screen that allows access to Security. Although there are no vents in Security, a vent is at the exit of the room.

Specimen Room

Specimen Room is a medium-sized, hexagonal room located near the bottom right of the map. To access it, players must pass through one of the small Decontamination rooms from either Admin or Laboratory, then walk through a long, empty hallway. The room features windows that let players see Outside from within.

Storage

Storage is a small, isolated room with only a few boxes and a red gas canister, which is once again used to refill the fuel can during the Fuel Engines task. There is also a hole there, which is connected to the holes in Office and the one near Communications. A window allows players to see both into and out of Storage depending on where they stand, located at the south of the room.

Weapons

On Polus, Weapons is a medium-sized room with two tasks: Clear Asteroids and Download Data. The room is inside a pod, located at the bottom of the Polus base, with an entrance at the top leading to Outside (the part near Communications). The Download Data panel is on a short wall perpendicular to the pod's entrance (on the upper right side). Clear Asteroids is in the top-right corner and is accessed via a PC on a desk, which fires a laser outside the pod into outer space. There is a vent right outside the exit of the room. Impostors can close the doors of Weapons on Polus, unlike The Skeld.

The Airship

Armory

Armory is a large room shaped like a backward "C". The floor is made out of wood in the upper section, with weapons displayed on the wall. There is also an exit that leads to a hallway. This hallway connects to Cockpit, Communications, and Engine Room. The lower section has a purple carpet with yellow outlines as the floor, with more weapons displayed on the wall, and an exit leading to Kitchen.

Brig

Brig is a small, rectangular room with three cell doors that cannot be opened. To the left is Vault, to the right is Gap Room, and at the bottom is a hallway which leads to Engine Room. The lower hallway also contains an Upload Data panel, the only task in Brig.

Cargo Bay

Cargo Bay is a large room located on the far-right side of the map. There are three exits: the top exit leading to Lounge, the bottom leading to Medical, and the left leading to the right side of Ventilation. Near the right exit is a vent and a security camera, and near the top exit are panels for Upload Data and Fix Wiring as well as another panel for Fix Lights. Near the top-right is a fuel tank for Fuel Engines, and near the bottom-right is the safe used for Unlock Safe.

Cockpit

Cockpit consists of several computers, a Divert Power panel, and an Upload Data panel in the front. There is also a table with a chair, a vent in the center, which connects to the ones in Viewing Deck and Vault, and on the top-left side, there is an Admin map.

Electrical

On The Airship, Electrical is made up of many small rooms, each connected with randomly-opened doors making it like a maze. The doors will always be opened in a pattern that allows the player to reach any exit from any other exit or at the very least allow all rooms to be accessible. There are three exits to Electrical. The left exit, which has a hallway leading to Security; the top exit, with a ladder leading to Main Hall; and the right exit, which leads to Medical.

Engine Room

Engine Room is a large, long room with exits to the left, above, and right. A crane is positioned in the top-right corner and two engines to the left side of the room. Sets of stairs are positioned at the top and right entrances and near the bottom of the room. At the bottom staircase lies a vent and a panel for Fuel Engines. The rightmost exit leads to Main Hall, and the leftmost exit leads to a short hallway connecting Armory, Communications, and Cockpit. The hallway above linking to Brig is also a part of Engine Room. However, even though the Download Data panel is located there, the task shows that the panel is in Brig.

Gap Room

On the left side of Gap Room, there is a ladder which goes up to Meeting Room, and a door which leads to Brig. Next to the ladder is a keypad, and further down on the same side is a vent, which leads to the lower part of the right side in the Gap Room and the upper vent in Main Hall. On the right side, there is a Download Data panel and a door that leads to Records. There is also a ladder going down, leading to a keypad, a panel to Accept Diverted Power, and a vent that leads to the left side of the Gap Room and the upper vent in Main Hall. There is a moving platform that can be used to cross.

Hall of Portraits

Hall of Portraits is a hallway with several portraits and a red carpet that connects Kitchen and Security. It has various pictures of people in the Crewmate style.

Kitchen

Kitchen has a blue and white floor pattern and consists of a few tables with dishes and knives, another cuisine, and a sink. There is also a vent, a fridge, and three doors. The top-left exit leads to Armory, the bottom-left exit leads to Viewing Deck, and the right exit leads to Hall of Portraits, which connects to Security.

Lounge

Lounge is comprised of two connected rooms: one with pool tables and another with bathroom stalls. The room containing pool tables has purple carpet and two pool tables in the center. Hung on the back wall are cue sticks, dartboards, and a Fix Wiring panel. The back-left exit connects to Records, while the front-right exit connects to the stalls.

The room with stalls has a tiled floor, four bathroom stalls with toilets and plungers, and an Empty Garbage bin near the exit to the pool tables. Each stall is closed with a green door, which can be opened by using it.

Main Hall

Main Hall consists of six rooms and a corridor connecting them. Three of these rooms appear to be bedrooms, one of which contains a ladder leading to Electrical and another which contains a vent. This vent leads to the vents in Engine Room and Kitchen. There is also a room full of cleaning supplies, which contains a vent that leads to either side of Gap Room, a room which contains the pipe used to Decontaminate, and a darkroom for completing Develop Photos. The left exit of this room leads to Engine Room, while the right leads to Showers.

Medical

Medical is divided into two sections. The right section (which is presumably a waiting room) has several chairs; a couple of tables - one with two water dispensers, a stack of paper cups, and scattered magazines on and around it - and the other table with a magazine on top of it as well; and the Empty Garbage task. The left section is C-shaped and is larger than the right section. It contains a panel for Download Data, a weighing scale, and two beds - with the Vitals monitor located in between them.

Meeting Room

Meeting Room is a large, vaguely rectangular room divided into three sections. The left section has a security camera on the left wall, a panel for Empty Garbage, and a ladder leading down to Gap Room. There is a large table with the emergency button, surrounded by several blue chairs in the middle section. A panel for Accept Diverted Power and Fix Wiring can also be seen. The right section has some chairs and a panel for Enter Id Code.

Outside

On The Airship, there are two Outside locations:

one accessed from Viewing Deck, and another from Security. From Viewing Deck, Outside is a small, square balcony with a wooden plank extending from the balcony. From Security, Outside is a larger balcony with stairs leading down to the right. At both locations, there is a panel for Upload Data, and The Airship's engine can be heard as clouds move by.

Records

Records is a large, circular room consisting of four bookshelves, a table with a file and some paper, and two brown chairs surrounding the table. It has three exits. The left exit leads to Gap Room, the right exit leads to Lounge, and the bottom exit leads to Showers.

Security cameras can also be seen by the left and right exits.

Security

On The Airship, Security is a small room with two entrances. It consists of a monitor to use Security and a few computers; the rightmost one being a panel used for Rewind Tapes. At the bottom, there is an exit to a balcony, similar to Viewing Deck.

Showers

Showers is a medium-sized room with benches and 15 towels lying around, with some showers by the upper-right area. In the right area with benches is a vent leading to Records and Cargo Bay. Showers has three exits: one to the left connected to Main Hall, a second at the top connecting to Records, and a third to the right that is connected to the left side of Ventilation.

Vault

Vault is a large, circular room divided into four sections. It is located near the top left of the map. Two of these sections have two mannequins each, while the other two have gold and artifacts in them, being a golden gun and a red halo energy sword, respectively. In the middle of all the sections, the Romanian Ruby from The Henry Stickmin Collection is on display, used for completing the Polish Ruby task. There is a vent on the left, which leads directly to the one in Cockpit and a camera attached to the door on the right, which leads to Brig.

Ventilation

Ventilation is a small room with two different platforms separated by a huge gap, which cannot be crossed. The two platforms are at different heights. The left side of Ventilation is accessed from Showers, and the right side is accessed from Cargo Bay.

Viewing Deck

Viewing Deck is a small room with four windows looking outside and a platform going outside. Inside is a vent, which leads directly to the one in Cockpit, Download Data and Fix Wiring panels, and a Fix Lights panel. The right exit connects to Kitchen, while the front exit connects to Outside.

Trivia

  • Security, Admin, and Lower Decontamination on Polus and Hall of Portraits on The Airship have no Tasks.
  • On The Skeld, every location has at least two tasks.
  • The Airship has the most locations of any Among Us map to date, with twenty total.
