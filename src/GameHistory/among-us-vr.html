Among Us VR

Among Us VR is an upcoming first-person 3D spin-off of Among Us for virtual reality headsets. It will include most mechanics from the main game, but only includes The Skeld map. It is incompatible with the main game and can only be played with other Among Us VR players. On April 20, 2022, it was announced that Among Us VR will be released during Holiday 2022.
