Amy Liu, known online as Aemu, is an artist at Innersloth born on June 11. Victoria Tran

Victoria Tran, also known as TheVTran online, is the community director at Innersloth and resides in Canada. Her job is handling most of the community communications, talking to everyone, and streamlining the flow of information to Innersloth's developers. This clears up time for them to work on Among Us, priorities, improvements, etc. She manages the official Among Us and Innersloth Twitter accounts. She also probably runs the other Among Us social media accounts, including TikTok and Instagram.
